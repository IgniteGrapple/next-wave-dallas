The label

What is Next Wave Dallas?

Next Wave Dallas is a hip hop and trap-specific nonprofit record label. We invest in artists the system left behind by pairing fair music agreements with real-life support: housing, therapy, and higher education.

Mission

Empower the next wave of hip hop artists through healing, housing, and higher education— without violence, without exploitation.

Vision

A world where trap and R&B aren’t tools of survival, but platforms for healing, ownership, and lasting change starting in Dallas.

Core values
  • Anti-violence & pro-autonomy.
  • Artists retain ownership of their work (max 5% royalty back to the org).
  • Trauma-informed care rooted in criminology and re-entry work.
  • No religious angle—fully secular support.
