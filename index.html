The label

Hip hop isn’t the problem. It’s the path out.

Next Wave Dallas is a hip hop and trap-specific nonprofit record label based in Dallas. We combine artist development with housing, mental health, and higher education in one organization.

Mission

Empower the next wave of hip hop artists through healing, housing, and higher education— without violence, without exploitation.


Vision

A world where trap and R&B aren’t tools of survival, but platforms for healing, ownership, and lasting change starting in Dallas.

Core values
  • Anti-violence & pro-autonomy.
  • Artists retain ownership of their work.
  • Trauma-informed, justice-focused care.
  • No religious angle—fully secular support.
Programs

Two ways into the Next Wave.

In-house housing for artists who need stability. Outpatient support for artists already in the community. Same expectations: renounce violence, show up, do the work.

In-house (residential)
  • Transitional / supportive housing in Dallas.
  • Mandatory renouncement of violence and drill-crew activity.
  • Therapy, anger management, MRT and psychiatry.
  • Full-time social worker for case management.
Outpatient (community)
  • Outreach social worker for artists not living on-site.
  • Support with probation terms, court dates and employment.
  • Help preparing for shows, releases, interviews and grants.
  • Pathway to earn housing through progress and consistency.

Education
Dallas College partnership in progress: GED support plus Commercial Music A.A.S. tracks in Music Business, Performing Musician, Producer & Songwriter, and Recording Technology.
For artists

Who we’re built for.

Trap and R&B artists and producers with real stories who are ready to leave violence behind and build careers with support—not exploitation.

You might be a fit if…
  • You make hip hop / trap / R&B and take your craft seriously.
  • You’re navigating probation, court, or unstable housing.
  • You’re willing to work in a non-violent, accountability-based space.
  • You’re open to therapy, coaching and honest feedback.
What you get
  • Respectful studio access, producers and engineers.
  • Case management, mental-health support and housing pathways.
  • Education options: GED and Commercial Music degrees.
  • Shows, press and brand support aligned with your story.

Year one launches with a pilot cohort of 3–5 Dallas artists and a collaborative project (EP or mixtape) that doubles as a fundraiser and proof of concept.

Partners

Courts, funders and industry at the same table.

Next Wave Dallas is designed so probation, public defenders, foundations and music partners can plug in without compromising safety or artist ownership.

Courts & probation
  • Alternative-to-incarceration and re-entry placements.
  • Clear reporting on attendance, milestones and progress.
  • Secure, staffed housing for select clients.
Funders & industry
  • Underwrite beds, therapy hours and tuition.
  • Support key staff: social workers, lawyer, security, admin.
  • Studios, producers, venues and publicists who respect artist autonomy.

The funding model blends donations, grants, a modest royalty cap (up to 5% back to the org), merch and performances—so the label is sustainable without ever robbing the artists.
