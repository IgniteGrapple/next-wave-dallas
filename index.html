Founders & Mission

Built by Paige Smith, M.S. (criminology & probation) and Kevin Almond, MBA (music + business). A label + housing + education pipeline combined.

What we focus on
  • Nonviolent artist development

Who we serve
  • Trap & R&B artists

Mission & Vision
