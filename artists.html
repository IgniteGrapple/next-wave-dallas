For artists

Who we’re built for.

Next Wave Dallas is designed for trap and R&B artists and producers with real stories who are ready to leave violence behind and build careers with support, not exploitation.

You might be a fit if…
  • You create hip hop / trap / R&B and treat it as a serious craft.
  • You’re navigating probation, court, re-entry or unstable housing.
  • You’re willing to work in a non-violent, accountable environment.
  • You’re open to therapy, coaching and honest feedback.
What you get
  • Respectful studio access, producers and engineers.
  • Case management, mental-health support and housing pathways.
  • Education options: GED and Commercial Music A.A.S. degrees.
  • Guidance on shows, press, branding and public voice.
Pilot roster

Year one launches with a small cohort of 3–5 Dallas artists working on a joint project (EP or mixtape) that raises funds and proves the model.
