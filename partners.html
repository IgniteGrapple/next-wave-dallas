Partners

Courts, funders and industry at the same table.

Next Wave Dallas is structured so probation, public defenders, foundations and music partners can plug in without compromising safety or artist ownership.

Courts & probation
  • Alternative-to-incarceration and re-entry placements.
  • Clear reporting on program attendance and milestones.
  • Secure, supervised housing for eligible clients.
Funders & donors
  • Underwrite beds, therapy hours and tuition costs.
  • Support key roles: social workers, lawyer, security, admin.
  • Back the first cohort and inaugural label project.
Industry & brands
  • Studios, producers and engineers offering fair, transparent deals.
  • Venues for sober showcases and community events.
  • Publicists and branding mentors who center empowerment, not exploitation.
Funding model (high level)

Long-term sustainability blends donations, grants, a modest royalty cap (up to 5% back to the org), merch and performances—so the label can last without ever robbing the artists it serves.
