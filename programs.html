Programs

Housing, healing and higher education.

We run two main tracks: in-house housing for artists who need stability, and outpatient support for artists already in the community. Both tracks connect to education and career pathways in music.

In-house (residential)
  • Transitional or supportive housing in Dallas.
  • Mandatory renouncement of violence and drill crew activity.
  • Therapy, anger management, MRT and psychiatry.
  • Full-time in-house social worker for case management.
Outpatient (community)
  • Outreach social worker for artists who live off-site.
  • Support with probation terms, court dates and paperwork.
  • Help with employment prep, transportation and crisis response.
  • Coaching around shows, releases, interviews and grants.
Education

In partnership with Dallas College, artists can work toward a GED or Commercial Music A.A.S. degrees in:

  • Music Business & Entrepreneurship
  • Performing Musician
  • Producer & Songwriter
  • Recording Technology
